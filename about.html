About Us

Trident is a solid and reliable reseller focused on sourcing and delivering physical commodities including metals and bulk products. Trading is the engine of our business. In fact, almost all of the Tridents activities are driven by trading â€“ from exploration to shipping and storage all over the world. More than simply a thread linking every aspect of what we do, trading is the conduit through which we receive and share vital market information, maintain the balance and flow of our liquidity, and forge lasting, rewarding relationships with our customers all over the world.

With vast trading experience Trident is a limited company based in Hong Kong. Our team has over 30 years experience in metal trading. Over the years Trident has developed an extensive network of important stakeholders all over the world. Trident is focused on sourcing and delivering physical commodities such as precious and base metals. We also provide market information, project consultation and other client-specific industry-related services. Our commercial counterparts are producers, traders and industrial consumers. Our role is to be a reliable and competitive partner in the global markets we serve, and to support our corporate partners in their own expansion and development. Tridents management team provides client-partners the benefits of working with experienced industry professionals. Backgrounds in physical products and trading, physical product transportation, port configuration and management, contract negotiation and risk assessment ensures both client-partners and Trident's experience truly professional synergy in the supply- demand fulfillment process.

Trident can trade virtually round-the-clock due to our extensive contacts and market knowledge. Trident also provides the following trading options:

  • Hedging
  • Arbitrage
  • Trading for speculative or investment purposes
